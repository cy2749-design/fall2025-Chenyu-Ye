Morning thoughts

Scheduled to be delivered on December 25, 2024.

To

Sarah Miller

Written on August 15, 2023

Letter Content

Dear Sarah,

I'm writing this in the morning, after everything that happened last night. I thought a lot before putting these words down.

Sometimes I react too quickly in the moment, and I say things I don't really mean, or I don't say the things I actually want to say. This letter is my attempt to slow down and really think about what matters.

What I want you to know is that our friendship means everything to me. I know we both have strong feelings, and sometimes those feelings collide. But that doesn't change how much I care about you.

I've been reflecting on what happened, and I realize that my hurt was showing up as anger. I should have found a better way to express what I was feeling instead of letting it explode. That wasn't fair to you.

I'm not asking you to accept everything right away, or even at all. I just wanted you to know that I see you, I hear you, and I want to understand. Maybe we both needed space to let the dust settle.

This letter won't solve everything, but I hope it shows you that I'm trying. I'm trying to be better, to be gentler with my words, and to give us both the time we need.

I'm sending this because I believe in us. And because sometimes love means choosing your words carefully.

Yours,
